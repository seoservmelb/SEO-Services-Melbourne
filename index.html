1. Title Tags

Don’t forget to incorporate relevant keywords in your title tags so your pages can rank higher in search results.
2. Meta Descriptions
Meta descriptions are the short descriptions that appear under your page's title in search results. Although they don't directly affect your rankings, they're still important because they can influence whether or not people click through to your page. So, ensure your meta descriptions are interesting and informative and include relevant keywords to increase your chances of getting clicked.

Image alt text is the text that appears in place of an image if the image can't be displayed for some reason. Search engines also use it to understand what an image is about. So ensure that you include relevant keywords in your image alt text to help your images rank higher in image search results.
4. Anchor Text
Anchor text is the visible text used for hyperlinks for SEO Melbourne purposes. It's often one of the most abused ranking factors because people used to stuff keywords into their anchor text to manipulate search results. However, this doesn't work anymore and can result in penalties from Google. So just use natural-sounding anchor text relevant to the page you're linking to.
